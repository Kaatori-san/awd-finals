Return Policy

How to request a refund?

To request refund, send an email to salesgtz@gmail.com with the following subject and details

SUBJECT : Refund Request

BODY : Order Number, Refund Amount, Customer Name

A GTZ Online Sales Representative will call you within 24 hours upon receipt of the email to process additional details needed for the refund.

We understand the importance of a hassle-free refund process. To ensure complete transparency, we have outlined the estimated refund lead times for different payment methods. Please note that these timelines may vary slightly depending on your payment provider or bank's processing time. Rest assured, we are committed to processing your refund as quickly as possible

Payment Type Processing days
Cash/ Cash on Delivery 2-3 business days
E-Wallet 2-3 business days
BillEase 2-3 business days
Debit Cards/ Bank Deposits 3-5 business days
Credit Cards 7-14 days depending on servicing bank

How to return an item?

Before you return an item, please coordinate first with us thru our GTZ page or email us at supportgtz@gmail.com and we will provide instruction on how to process item return.
