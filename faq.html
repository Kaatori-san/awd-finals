FAQs

How to order

  1. Add item to your cart.
  2. Click on the Cart icon in the upper right corner to view your cart.
  3. Click on the Checkout button.
  4. Enter Billing and Shipping Details.
  5. Select a payment option.
  6. Click on Confirm Order Button.

How do I receive my order?

Orders can be received via delivery.

Shipping

Shipping is currently available for Metro Manila only.

How to track my order

  • Log in and go to "My Account" to access your dashboard.
  • Check the order history to see your order status.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, send an email to salesgtz@gmail.com with the following details:

SUBJECT: Refund Request

BODY: Order Number, Refund Amount

An GTZ Online Sales Representative will contact you within 24 hours.
